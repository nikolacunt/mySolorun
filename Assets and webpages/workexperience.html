My work experience.

Working as a graphic designer for prominent pages like 28. Jun and LeagueAlerts on Instagram has been a transformative experience in my professional journey. These roles have allowed me to hone my creative skills while contributing to brands with significant online presence. At 28. Jun, I focused on creating visually engaging content that resonated with a diverse audience. This role taught me the importance of understanding target demographics and crafting designs that not only capture attention but also convey the brand's message effectively.
Similarly, my work with LeagueAlerts involved designing graphics that appealed to a passionate gaming community. Here, I learned to balance aesthetic appeal with functionality, ensuring that information was easily digestible while still maintaining a vibrant visual style. Both experiences have deepened my appreciation for the power of visual communication and strengthened my ability to work collaboratively in fast-paced environments. As I continue to grow in this field, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and the potential to create impactful designs that resonate with audiences.
